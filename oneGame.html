Welcome to our town - Weatherton. Your mission is to help us work with climate adaptation.

The aim of this game is to understand how society can adapt to climate change. The game requires no preparation and takes approximately 30-45 minutes to play. If you want to play as a group, we recommend a discussion about your experiences of adapting the city.

Serious gaming is a tool for analyzing complex contexts. It helps visualize background and causations for a phenomenon. Hopefully, the game will create curiosity and interest in climate change adaptation.

The causes and effects of climate change can be studied further.

Adaptation and politics discussions can be used in social studies lessons. Topics may include economics, ethics, and technical solutions for building a sustainable society.

More about climate change and adaptation at SMHI.se and the Swedish portal.
